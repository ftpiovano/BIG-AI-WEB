# Initial Concept

The project aims to update a brownfield marketing website by integrating content and UX patterns for automotive and AI offerings. This involves evolving the existing site at https://www.bigintelligenceai.com/ with inspiration from https://emaginereality.com/, while maintaining the BIG brand identity. The focus is on improving the scroll experience, mobile-first design, performance, and accessibility, with new content related to Software Defined Vehicles (SDV), AI in software development, vehicle diagnostics, and AI-driven automation/voice AI offerings.

## Brand Context

### Brand Voice
Concise, confident, engineering-first

### Target Buyers
Automotive OEMs, Tier-1s, mobility/EV startups

### Primary CTA
Book a call / Start a project

### Design Direction
Modern, premium, high-trust; motion subtle; strong typography; generous whitespace

### SEO Basics
Keywords around SDV, OTA, diagnostics, AI in automotive software, voice AI automation
